\# Business Insights



\## Key Findings

\- A small subset of customers contributes a disproportionately large share of total revenue.

\- Certain product categories generate higher average order values but lower purchase frequency.

\- Clear cross-selling opportunities exist among frequently co-purchased products.



\## Business Impact

\- Revenue concentration increases exposure to churn risk if high-value customers disengage.

\- Category-level performance variance highlights opportunities for pricing and bundling optimization.

\- Effective cross-selling strategies can directly increase average order value and overall revenue.



\## Recommendations

\- Prioritize retention initiatives for high lifetime value customers.

\- Test bundled offerings in high-margin product categories.

\- Leverage purchase pattern analysis to drive targeted cross-sell promotions.
